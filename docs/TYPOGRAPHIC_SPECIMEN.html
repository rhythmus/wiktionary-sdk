έγραψα 1st pers. singular perfective past of:
γρά·φω /ˈɣra.fo/ v. (verb), pres. (conjug.) γράφω, γράψω (aor. έγραψα), γέγραφα (perf. γέγραμμαι)
Etymology (← IE gʰerbh- ‘to scratch, carve, write’ ← PIE gerbʰ-; cognate with OHG kerban ‘to carve’, ON kroppa)
  • 1. [TRANS.] To write, compose (lit. work): write a letter (γράφω ἐπιστολήν).
  • 2. [FIG.] To record, register, list (as in a register).
  • 3. [ADMIN.] To draft (a document or law).
  • 4. [MED.] (archaic) To prescribe (a remedy).
  • 5. [LAW ENFORC.] (for traffic violations, etc) To ticket, to issue a fine: με έγραψαν (they ticketed me).
SYN: χαράσσω, τυπώνω; ANT: σβήνω, διαγράφω; DER: συγγραφέας, ἐπιγραφή.
